iPhone 11 Pro Summary

iPhone 11 Pro smartphone was launched on 10th September 2019.

  • 5.80-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1125x2436 pixels at a pixel density of 458 pixels per inch (ppi).
  • Powered by a hexa-core Apple A13 Bionic processor with 4GB of RAM.
  • Runs iOS 13 and is powered by a 3046mAh non-removable battery
  • Supports wireless charging, as well as proprietary fast charging.

Galaxy Note10 | Note10+

Galaxy Note10 was launched on 7th August 2019.

  • 6.30-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1080x2280 pixels at a pixel density of 401 pixels per inch (ppi)
  • Powered by a 1.9GHz octa-core Samsung Exynos 9825 processor that features 4 cores clocked at 1.9GHz, 2 cores clocked at 2.4GHz and 2 cores clocked at 2.7GHz with 8GB of RAM
  • Runs Android 9 Pie and is powered by a 3500mAh battery and supports wireless charging.
